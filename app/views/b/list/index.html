  1. Digital Marketplace

Find cloud hosting, software and support

  1. 1. Save a search for cloud services

    Choose a category, then search for services using keywords and filters. Save your search terms so you can edit them later. Your search terms should be based on your requirements.

    • Search for cloud services Log in to your account to see a list of your saved searches

  2. 2. Download your search results

    When you’ve finished editing your search terms, download a spreadsheet of your results to help you review or compare services.

  3. 3. Review services

    Review and compare services to find the one that best meets your budget and requirements. You can contact the suppliers to ask questions, but you must not negotiate with them about the details of their service.

  4. 4. Award a contract

    When you have chosen the winning service, follow the guidance on how to award a contract. Check whether you need to publish the contract. You also need to complete the Customer Benefits Record form.

    Tell us if you bought a service or not. Knowing the outcome of your saved search helps us to improve the Digital Marketplace.
